ROME

Rome, Italy's capital, is a captivating blend of ancient history and modern vitality. It stands as a living museum, with iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon offering glimpses into its glorious past. The Vatican City, an independent enclave within the city, houses St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, drawing countless pilgrims and art enthusiasts. Rome's bustling streets are lined with charming cafes, designer boutiques, and world-class restaurants, while its rich cultural heritage, displayed in its art, architecture, and cuisine, continues to mesmerize visitors from around the globe.
