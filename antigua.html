ANTIGUA

Antigua and Barbuda, a Caribbean island nation, beckon with 365 pristine beaches, inviting azure waters, and lush landscapes. Steeped in history, it features Nelson's Dockyard, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and Shirley Heights, offering panoramic views and vibrant cultural events. The island's rich heritage blends African, British, and Caribbean influences, evident in its music, dance, and cuisine. Antigua is a tropical haven that seamlessly marries natural beauty with a captivating cultural tapestry, making it a sought-after destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration.
