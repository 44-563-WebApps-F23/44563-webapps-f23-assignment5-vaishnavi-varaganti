VARANASI

Varanasi, India's spiritual epicenter, is famed for its sacred Ghats along the Ganges River, where rituals and the enchanting Ganga Aarti unfold daily. It hosts iconic temples like the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, drawing countless devotees. This ancient city weaves a cultural tapestry rich in classical arts, music, and silk production. Varanasi's Banaras Hindu University is a bastion of education and heritage. A timeless city, Varanasi embodies India's spiritual and cultural essence.
